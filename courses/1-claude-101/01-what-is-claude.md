- Name the principles that guide its design

## What is Claude?

Claude is an AI assistant — more than a chatbot, it's a thinking partner for complex work. Claude is powerful, helpful, harmless, and honest. It's designed to be a tool for thinking partners, not a replacement for human judgment.

**Key takeaways:**
- Claude is helpful, harmless, and honest. At a high level, Claude is guided by principles that help it avoid harmful content, remain honest, and stay focused on being helpful.
- Claude is more than a chatbot — Claude is capable of a wide variety of conversations and text processing tasks while maintaining a high degree of nuance. You can use Claude for writing, analysis, coding, and much more.
- Before you start a conversation with Claude, consider writing the stage-setting prelude. By addressing the stage (context), defining the task, and specifying rules (format and style), you give Claude what it needs to produce exactly what you're looking for.
- The real power of Claude comes with a consistent and frequent collaboration practice.

## Ways to access Claude

- Claude.ai (web, desktop, and mobile apps) — Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise plans
- Claude Apps: Claude Code, Claude Cowork, Claude for Chrome, Claude for Slack, Claude for Excel
